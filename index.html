Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work?

Your first lesson is completely free with no obligation. Your child will join a real class, receive homework, and get a full experience of our teaching style.

What are class sizes?

Maximum 6-8 students per class. This ensures every student gets individual attention and can ask questions freely.

How is pricing structured?

We have three tiers: Junior (Years 7-8) from $80/week, Intermediate (Years 9-10) from $100/week, and Senior (Years 11-12) from $120/week. Multi-subject discounts apply automatically.

Can I change subjects or cancel?

Yes. You can switch subjects at any time. We require 2 weeks notice for cancellations. See our refund policy for details.

Where are classes held?

Our centre is in Epping, Sydney. We also offer online classes for students who prefer remote learning.
