Refund Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Our commitment: We want you to be completely satisfied with BEAM Academy. If you're not happy, we'll work with you to make it right.

Trial Lessons

Your first lesson is always free with no obligation. If you decide BEAM Academy isn't the right fit after your trial, you won't be charged anything.

Refund Eligibility

Situation Refund
Withdrawal with 2+ weeks' notice Full refund of unused fees
Withdrawal with 1-2 weeks' notice 50% refund of unused fees
Withdrawal with less than 1 week's notice No refund
BEAM Academy cancels a class Full credit or refund for that lesson
Student absence (24+ hours' notice) Make-up lesson offered
Student absence (less than 24 hours' notice) No refund or make-up

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund or discuss your situation:

  • Email us at [email protected] with your name and details
  • Or call us at 0491 791 447

We aim to process all refund requests within 5-7 business days. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.

Make-Up Lessons

If you notify us at least 24 hours before a scheduled lesson that you cannot attend, we will offer a make-up lesson subject to availability. Make-up lessons must be used within the same term and cannot be accumulated or transferred.

Service Dissatisfaction

If you're unhappy with our tutoring services for any reason, please contact us. We value feedback and will work with you to address any concerns. In cases where we cannot resolve the issue to your satisfaction, we may offer a partial or full refund at our discretion.

Exceptional Circumstances

We understand that unexpected situations arise. If you need to withdraw due to exceptional circumstances (illness, family emergency, relocation), please contact us. We will consider each case individually and may offer more flexible refund terms.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Need Help?

If you have questions about refunds or need to discuss your situation, we're here to help.
